# Hype Man Twitter Bot
@-mention its Twitter account; it replies as your hype man (if it's paying attention)

## If ya don't know, now ya know:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hype_man

A hype man in hip hop music and rapping is a backup rapper and/or singer who supports the primary rappers with exclamations and interjections, and who attempts to increase the audience's excitement with call-and-response chants.

Sayings the bot can choose from:
* CHYEAH!
* It's ya boy!
* WHO RUN THIS?
* Put your hands up!
* Hands up!
* GO!
* ...hopefully better ones to come


[Based on the excellent Twitter bot library Chatterbot]
